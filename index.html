Emily Roberts, PhD

  Assistant Professor

  Department of Biostatistics

  College of Public Health

  University of Iowa


Hi! I am an assistant professor of biostatistics at the University of Iowa. I completed my PhD at the Department of Biostatistics, University of Michigan in 2022 with my PhD dissertation “Causal Inference Methods and Intermediate Endpoints in Randomized Clinical Trials”. I’m excited about statistical methodology that can be meaningfully used in a clinical setting. Welcome to my page where I have included a bit about myself and my work.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude in 2016 from Coe College with a BA in mathematics and psychology as a member of Phi Beta Kappa National Honor Society. Additionally, I earned my MS in biostatistics from Michigan in 2018. My passion for medical research and ultimate career goals stem from my diagnosis of type I diabetes at the age of four.
